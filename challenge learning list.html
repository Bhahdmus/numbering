Water makes up approximately 55-60% of an adult human's body weight, varying depending on factors like age, sex, and body composition.

Here's a breakdown of water's presence in the human body:

 Body Composition:
  • Adult men: 55-60% water
  • Adult women: 50-55% water
  • Infants: 70-80% water
  • Elderly: 45-50% water
Interesting Facts:
  1. The human brain can survive without water for 1 week.
  2. Water helps maintain blood pressure.
  3. Water supports immune function.
